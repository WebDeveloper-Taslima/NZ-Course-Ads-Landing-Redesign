Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our courses

What is the format of the courses?

Our courses are conducted in a workshop format over two consecutive days. They include a mix of presentations, interactive exercises, role-playing scenarios, and group discussions to ensure practical learning and skill application.

Are there any prerequisites for attending?

No specific prerequisites are required. Our courses are designed to accommodate professionals at all levels, from entry-level to senior management. We only ask that participants come with an open mind and willingness to engage.

Do you offer corporate group bookings?

Yes, we offer customized corporate training solutions for teams of 5 or more. We can tailor the content to address specific organizational needs and can deliver the training at your premises or a venue of your choice. Contact us for group rates.

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer a full refund for cancellations made up to 14 days before the course date. For cancellations between 7 and 14 days before the course, we offer a 50% refund. Unfortunately, we cannot offer refunds for cancellations made less than 7 days before the course, but you may transfer your registration to another person.
